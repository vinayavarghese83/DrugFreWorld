Free educational materials about the most commonly abused drugs. Download or request printed copies.

EDUCATIONAL BOOKLETS THAT SAVE LIVES

These easy-to-read booklets provide factual information about the most commonly abused drugs. They describe how the drugs work, their short-term and long-term effects, the myths surrounding them, and the warning signs of abuse.

These materials are designed for young people, but parents, teachers, counselors, police, and health professionals can all use them to educate others about drugs.
